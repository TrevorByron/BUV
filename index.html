About BUV

Since 2012, BUV Tanzania has been providing transportation vehicles for rural Africa. The strategy of BUV Tanzania is to focus on excellent customer service through our industry leading driver-training program. These drivers emphasize service above self and the profits follow. Our manufacturing facility is located at SIDO (Small Industrial Development Organization), Arusha, Tanzania. The BUV provides opportunities for people to increase their economic status.
